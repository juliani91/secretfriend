Secret Friend Generator

How it works?

The user has to select how many participants are going to be (maximum 10). Once this step is done he/she needs to input their emails and names on the boxes. Next just click the send email button and every participant will receive in their emails who is their secret friend.

Make sure

Make sure to know what your secret friend wants for present. It will be a good idea to have a list with the desired presents. (This function will be implemented later).
